Pipelines for BMC Modeling in In Vitro Screening

A comparative study of biostatistical pipelines for benchmark concentration modeling of in vitro screening assays was published in Computational Toxicology in 2025.

The paper compares alternative statistical pipelines for concentration–response analysis, with an emphasis on reproducibility, uncertainty, and regulatory usability. A core concern is how modeling choices propagate into benchmark concentration estimates and downstream interpretation.
